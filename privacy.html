Privacy Policy

This game uses Patreon OAuth to verify your membership tier and unlock the Patreon build.

Purpose of Use

To verify Patreon membership and grant access to the Patreon build and to troubleshoot authentication issues.

Data Storage

Access tokens are used temporarily during verification and are not stored. We do not persist personal data.

Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Data is used solely to validate access.

Security

OAuth over HTTPS is used. We minimize data collection and retention.
